About

This is a B2B marketplace for sourcing made in the USA suppliers and manufacturing. Think of it like "Alibaba for the USA".

Problem: Many people order stuff from overseas simply because it's easy to search Alibaba for things like "Textiles". Outsourcing has lead to many issues.

Solution: We want to help businesses find trusted, US-based suppliers and factories to shorten supply chains and make them more reliable.
